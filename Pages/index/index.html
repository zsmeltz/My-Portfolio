About Me



Hey everyone, my name is Zach Smelcer. I was born in Columbia, MO and have lived in Missouri most of my life until this year. I grew up in a town outside of St. Louis called O'Fallon and lived there through my elementry, middle school, and high school days. Now, I live in Plainfield, IL and I love it here. COVID really hit me hard and I wish to be able to go out and experience the world again.

Before I moved here, I was in a metal band named "Soul Joke". Guitar was always a dream of mine to play and it was a great experience getting to play it live. These days I only play a little because I am more focused on becoming a Web Developer and I also play way too many video games.
